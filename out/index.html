How it works

Let’s say you’re heading out to pick up lunch. Maybe you’re getting a spicy Thai salad. Yum! Your friend and co-worker Sally wants one too, so you offer to get her one while you’re there. It costs $13.

Sally pays $15

Sally pays a little extra to have lunch delivered to her desk, while helping charity.

You get $13

You take a walk and buy Sally lunch with her $13 – the exact cost of the salad.

World Vision gets $2

You raise $2 to help those in need. You’re happy. Sally’s happy. Everyone’s happy!

Click by click

Sometimes you might be heading out to grab lunch, other times a co-worker might be picking it up. Either way, LunchHero makes getting lunch more fulfilling than ever. Here’s a click-by-click breakdown of how we make lunch the best meal of the day.

  • 1

    Ask your workmates if they want lunch on Slack

    the LunchHero bot is listening out for the word ‘lunch’

  • 2

    Grab their lunch when you’re picking up yours

    what a legend!

  • 3

    Ask them to pay using LunchHero

    so we can raise money for charity

LunchHero adds a $2 delivery fee that goes to World Vision

we take no money in this transaction. The lunch money is transferred to you immediately. The $2 is sent to World Vision daily (to avoid excessive transfer fees).

Why LunchHero?

Grabbing lunch for your workmates is a pretty nice thing to do right? And helping out those less fortunate is downright noble. As far as we can tell, that makes LunchHero a win-win. Oh, and you can also compete for glory!

For glory

Compete against your workmates for bragging rights.

Feel good

Get your fill at lunchtime by helping co-workers and the less fortunate.

Give back

It’s a super-simple way of making a real difference.

LunchHero has kicked off with World Vision as its launch charity. However, we will be adding new charities soon. If you’re a charity and you’re interested in taking donations through LunchHero, please sign up and we’ll be in touch when users can choose their own charity.
